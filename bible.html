Feed Your

Soul

God Can Help!

First of all, let me say this: Nobody loves you more than God. God's word is His way to fullfil us with all we need (1 Jo 4:9). Pretty sure u heard somewhere that our brain is the operating system of our body, so we should fullfil our brain with knwoledge (probably not exactly like that). That's true, the question is what tipe of knwoledge? I believe that God's word is the primier knwoledge we need. And it is available for everyone 24/7, u just need to look for it.

When reading the Bible, try to right down important contents and key word. Try also to have a dictionary by your side in case you don't know the meaning of a word and at least two version of the Bible, being one of then a physical one (I have four myself, just in case). Also find interesting books (biblical ones) of differnt topics to ensure u of God's will for that specif area of your life.

I suggest reading one chapter a day for the ones who doesn't have the habit, 'cause if u push too hard in the begining the probability is that u will be "lazy" quickly. So start small and consistent, and when u feel confortable, add one more chapter. Another suggestion, try to find a place where u feel comfortable, and set a specific time a day to read it. It will make your brain connect that especif place and time with your bible study and will be harder to forget about it.

Bible study envolves reading and meditating. That's why I said above to start small so u can read and meditate about what u reading. It won't take more than ten or fifteen minutes. U don't need a phisycal bible (even thought i strongly recommend), u can use your phone. There's a variety of Bible apps, u can choose the one tha bests suits u and your need. It can also be a great way to learn languages if u had both physical and digital Bible.

Something that a lot of people have dought is about salvation. No church, priest, pastor, nor actions can save. the Bible says:(...)12: Neither is there salvation in any other: for there is none other name under heaven given among men, whereby we must be saved. (Acts 4:10-12). Only Jesus can save u. God do not give u a cross heavier than the one u can actually carry. If u are here now, belive me is not an out of the blue situation, probably God wanted u here for a reason. So let me tell u a little something: You are an Overcomer.

Be able to love God is a gift, our life is a gift. When u are close to God u are fearless, painless, courage, and u belive that anything is possible, 'cause that's what God's word put in your heart. Because (...)23: And kings shall be thy nursing fathers, and their queens thy nursing mothers: they shall bow down to thee with their faces toward the earth, and lick up the dust of thy feet; and thou shalt know that I am the Lord: for they shall not be ashamed that wait for me.(Isaiah 49:22-23)

If u don't have the habit of reading the Bible i suggest asking friends who have the habit, and at least for the first two weeks read with them. Having someone who shares the habits and aspirations that u want to accomplish can be a great way of motivation and persistence. U will help each other and grow together.

Life Bible Gospel Spirituality

Lance Smith
